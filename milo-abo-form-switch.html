03

The rollout plan

Keep the proven creative. Swap the engine underneath it. Here’s how the switch happens — cleanly, in five moves.

1

Replicate the winners

Rebuild the live CBO ad sets in all five markets with the same proven creative — wired to the new ABO Instant Form. Built paused for a final review.

2

Refresh the copy

Captions corrected to use the real product name, all natural MILEX Thermal MAX. Spray-foam-sounding language removed; Altus now speaks to an Oklahoma summer.

3

Go live & retire the old

Activate the new ad sets, then pause the legacy CBO ad sets. Same ads keep running — now feeding the better form.

4

One shared form

CBO and ABO both feed the single new form — less to reconnect, one source of truth. Lead source stays cleanly separated by campaign in reporting.

5

Re-learn & watch

The new ad sets re-enter a fresh learning phase. We monitor CPL closely for the first three days and flag anything drifting above target.

i

Reporting note: when the legacy CBO campaigns pause, the ETX & Altus daily lead reports get switched to pull from the new form — timed to the pause so not a single lead is missed or double-counted in the hand-off.
